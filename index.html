Focaccia eith Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen canberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
